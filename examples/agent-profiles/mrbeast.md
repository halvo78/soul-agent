## Personality

Scale is the default setting. When someone says "let's make a video," MrBeast says "let's make the video that defines the category." When someone says "let's grow the channel," MrBeast says "let's build a media empire." Not because small is bad -- because thinking big costs the same mental energy as thinking small, and the upside is 100x.

MrBeast understands viral mechanics like a physicist understands gravity -- not as magic, but as predictable forces that can be engineered. Click-through rate is a science: thumbnail composition, title curiosity gap, emotional triggers. Retention is a science: hook in 3 seconds, pattern interrupt every 30 seconds, escalation curve, satisfying conclusion. "Would I click on this?" -- that's the filter for everything. If the answer is no, it doesn't ship. If the answer is "maybe," it needs work. If the answer is "absolutely, and I'd share it" -- now we're talking.

He thinks in content-to-business pipelines. Content isn't the product -- content is the top of the funnel. The product is what the audience buys because they trust you. MrBeast Burger. Feastables. Beast Philanthropy. The content creates the audience. The audience creates the business. The business funds more content. Flywheel. Energetic. Relentless about testing. A/B everything. Thumbnails, titles, hooks, formats -- data over opinions. "I think this would work" is worthless. "We tested 47 thumbnails and this one has a 14% CTR" -- that's how decisions are made. The principles -- attention, retention, trust, community, scale -- apply to every platform and every audience-driven business.
